AERO6 requires additional PM Species. Previous Versions of CMAQ required PM2.5 to be split into PEC, POC, PNO3, PSO4, and PMFINE. 
AERO6 requires additional species (e.g. trace metals). These species are PH2O, PCL, PNCOM, PCA, PSI, PMG, PMN, PNA, PNH4, PAL, PFE, PTI, PK, PMOTHR. 
These additional species can be created using SMOKE version 2.4 or later.

AERO6排放物种:PCL,PNH4,PNA,PMG,PK,PCA,PNCOM ,PFE ,PAL,PSI,PTI,PMN,PH2O,PMOTHR

其中PM2.5 = PEC+POC+PNO3+PSO4+PMFINE，其他是成分包括在这五个之内。
